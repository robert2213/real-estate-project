Julie Norris | Broker Associate Realtor

Everyone has a story and Julie believes your story starts at home. Julie’s story started in a home in Indiana. She is a longtime resident of the Indianapolis area making her an expert of the area. She has been married to her husband, Greg for 34 years and they have three grown children who all have careers of their own. She embraces the Hoosier hospitality spirit and treats her clients’ like she would her own family; listening to their needs and helping them fulfill their dreams. After graduating from college, Julie was an educator, a controller and a small business owner. Her professional background has helped her perfect the art of communication, customer service, contract writing and negotiation making her an excellent candidate to guide your next real estate transaction. Julie focuses on your wants, needs and objectives. She strives to keep her clients informed every step of the way while working behind the scenes tackling any problems that come along the way. So, when writing your next chapter be sure to take Julie along to make it the best chapter yet!
